**Kya bolein:**
> "Sir/Ma'am, ACKO ne Clause 4.1.2 (Specific Illnesses) ki waiting period apply ki. Lekin uss clause mein jo bimariyon ki list hai, usme 'Tight Phimosis with Balanitis with Meatal Stenosis' ka koi ullekh nahi hai. Waiting period sirf listed bimariyon pe lagti hai. Meri bimari list mein nahi hai, isliye waiting period hi apply nahi hoti."

**Ek line mein:** *"Jo list mein nahi, uske liye waiting period kaise?"*

### ✅ ARGUMENT 2: Medical Emergency thi, cosmetic nahi

**Kya bolein:**
